Ingredients:
  - cheddar cheese
  - ripe tomatoes
  - oregano (dried or fresh)
  - sliced bread

Directions:
  - slice tomatoes and place on a plate
  - slice or grate cheese and add to tomatoes
  - grill or microwave for 2 to 3 minutes
  - toast bread
  - spread cheese and tomato on the toast
  - add oregano as a garnish to taste
